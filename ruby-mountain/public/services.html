Construction services

Ruby Mountain offers a complete range of construction and maintenance services for private homes, lodges and commercial projects.

Construction

Turn-key construction of new private homes, lodges and outbuildings, including foundations, structure, roofing and finishes.

Building alterations

Additions, changes to layouts, new rooms and modernisation of existing buildings while keeping structural integrity and style in mind.

Civil works

Site clearing, earthworks, foundations, retaining walls, paving and stormwater solutions.

Project management

Planning, scheduling and coordination of all trades to keep your project on time and on budget.

General maintenance

Repair work, timber treatment, painting, waterproofing and other maintenance to protect your assets.

Rental of equipment and vehicles

Hire of construction equipment and vehicles, with operators where required, for project work.

Supply of labour

Provision of experienced construction teams for short-term and long-term projects.

Steel structures and storage units

Fabrication and construction of steel structures, sheds and storage facilities.
